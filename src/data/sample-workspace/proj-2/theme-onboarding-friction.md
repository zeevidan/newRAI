# Theme: Onboarding friction

**Status:** Validated  
**Owner:** Product research  
**Sources:** 6 interviews, 142 support tickets (90d)

## Problem statement

New CloudSuite customers take **3.2× longer** than benchmark to reach first successful integration sync, primarily due to SSO configuration and role mapping uncertainty.

## Evidence

| Signal | Volume | Severity |
|--------|--------|----------|
| SSO re-auth per environment | 4/6 interviews | High |
| Role confusion in admin UI | 5/6 interviews | Medium |
| "Stuck on onboarding" tickets | 38 tickets | High |

## Recommended bets

1. **Guided SSO wizard** with live validation
2. **Role templates** by industry vertical
3. **Onboarding agent** that reads config and flags gaps

## Success metrics

- Time-to-first-sync ↓ 40%
- Onboarding NPS ↑ 15 points
- Tier-2 support tickets ↓ 25%
